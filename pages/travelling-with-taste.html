Concept
Travelling With Taste is a goofy stealth game where you play as a suitcase full of rats. Steal cheese from the cheese-trafficking Airport Security, and escape before your plane takes off!
This game was created in Unreal Engine over the course of seven weeks by a team of seven developers.
Contributions
  • Managed a 7-person team using weekly sprints
  • Designed and programmed game UI
  • Prototyped, drafted, and tested level designs in engine
  • Programmed core game mechanics and props
Key Takeaways
  • Team Management and Sprint Methodology
  • In-Engine UI/UX Development
  • Level Design
