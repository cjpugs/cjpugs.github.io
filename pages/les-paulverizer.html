Concept
The Les Paulverizer website utilizes a physical Les Paulverizer device. This device is intended as a musical improv tool. It has four buttons and clips onto an instrument, so musicians can play looping background tracks to improvise to.
Our website interacts with a Les Paulverizer device, and allows users to upload their own music tracks. Then, they can adjust the tempo, time signature, and number of measures. The system then loops each track according to the user's needs.
